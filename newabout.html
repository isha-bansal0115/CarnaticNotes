About

Hello! I'm Bhavya Linga. I created this website because, as a flute player, I know how difficult it can be to play music in various foreign languages. Personally, I've had trouble playing Indian songs on the flute because the notes are in a different format than how I learned at school. My website will help with translating those Carnatic notes into Western notes, making it much easier to play these songs.
